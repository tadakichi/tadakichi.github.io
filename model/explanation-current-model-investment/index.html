Explanation of the current Model for investment

September 26, 2017

This time, I’m gonna explain the model I’m using for real investment. This model is the base model, so it will be the base when comparing with updates or changes.

Here is the simple diagram of the quant process.

Every process except evaluation is calculated in rebalancing period, and evaluation is calculated in monthly.

Differences of returns of each investment starting point are quite high, that’s because a stock(code: 002700) was or wasn’t included which recorded more than 200% returns for two months.

‘Average differences’ is the average difference with Kospi index monthly returns. Those are varied with investment start point. Please note that index numbers don’t consider related cost. ‘Hit ratio of differences’ is the probability of ‘Average differences’ higher than 0%.

And constraints of the model are here: -minimum differences with financial statements announcement time and investment time : 3 months -number of stocks invest: 20 -trading code: 0.33%(Korean market imposes a trading tax for 0.3%) -rebalancing period: 2 months
